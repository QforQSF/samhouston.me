About

Hey, I'm Sam.

I'm a community professional based in the SF Bay Area with a background spanning cybersecurity, startups, and gaming. I believe community is most powerful when it's treated as a bridge—connecting marketing, customer success, and support into something approachable and human.

My work lives at the intersection of strategy and execution: building programs that scale, onboarding experiences that drive value for customers and the business, and frameworks that make community legible to leadership.

Outside of work I'm with my wife and our son, enjoying live music, or adding new vinyl records to my collection.
